SOFTWARE DEVELOPMENT LIFE CYCLE

SDLC is a series of steps invoved in developing good software
SDLC fall under the project management area of software development

With SDLC;
Project managemnt happens in an organized & structured way
Cost of development is reduced
All stakeholders are given a chance to voice their opinions from the very beginning of the development
process
Help developers better understand what they are doing and why
Improved software quality
Shortened development cycles

SDLC is made of 7 stages:
Planning (requirement gathering & analyses)
Design 
Development (implementation & coding)
Testing
Deployment
Maintenance
