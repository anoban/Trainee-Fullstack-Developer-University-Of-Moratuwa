NEW YEAR CELEBRATIONS 2022

Start your new year with us!. Our institution Wildlyfe has organized a tree planting campaign on the new year day. We have collected over 10,000 seedlings and are looking for volunteers to plant them either in their homes or in a place where they will be well taken care of until they are grown enough to be on their own.

Join hands with us on new year day to save nature!, To make this earth greener!

Willing volunteers can collect the seedlings at our regional office in Jaffna, on No. 125, Hospital road, Jaffna overlooking the Jaffna Teaching Hospital. We will be available from 8.30 am to 6.30 pm

Once planted take a picture of the seedling and share in your socials with tags #GreenNewYear2022 #GoGreen #StopGlobalWarming, in order to encourage others to get involved.

Thank you for helping to make a difference!
